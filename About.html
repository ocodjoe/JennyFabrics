ABOUT

Fabrics that breathe life!

Hi!

My name is Jenny. I'm a plant biologist by profession and a fabrics enthusiast. I run JennyFabrics from St. Louis, MO, which is not only the home of toasted raviolis and gooey butter cakes but also where I met the love of my life.

My husband gets so many compliments on his brightly colored, African print shirts. Where could they get one? Well... they'd have to go all the way to Ghana.

I started JennyFabrics to provide Americans with high quality, West African wax print fabrics. My mother-in-law sources the most engaging prints in the clothing markets of Accra, Ghana.

For your next project, use one of our bright and beautiful, intricately designed cotton fabrics. I promise, you'll get compliments!
